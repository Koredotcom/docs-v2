# About Automation AI

Automation AI delivers agentic conversational experiences through DialogGPT and AI Agents, enabling human-like, multi-turn engagements that understand intent, retain context, and respond naturally. It empowers organizations to build sophisticated AI Agents that handle complex interactions, manage interruptions, and provide clarifications, creating personalized, self-service, human-like conversation experiences.
