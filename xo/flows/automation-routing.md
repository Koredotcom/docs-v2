
# Automation Routing Modes

Automation routing modes streamline the connection and configuration of automation apps, ensuring consistent and efficient workflows. All settings are located under **Flow & Channels** > **Automation Routing** > **Routing Modes** for easier access and management. The interface provides a quick view of **Chat** and **Voice** flows, displaying key routing details, including Flow Name, Automation Node, Routing Mode, and Channel. The integrated search bar enables easy filtering of results and efficient management of routing configurations.


Navigate to **Flow & Channels** > **Automation Routing** > **Routing Modes**. 
Click the three dots (more icons) for any welcome flow and select the required option: Automation Settings or Manage Linked Apps.

## Automation Settings

The settings allow you to configure Automation Node settings, including selecting the autonomy level for Automation AI, Automation AI options, Agentic App Configurations, connection rules, and error prompts. [Learn more](./node-types/automation.md).  

<img src="../images/automation-setting-with-message.png" alt="Automation Routing" title="Automation Routing" style="border: 1px solid gray; zoom:80%;">


## Manager Linked Apps

You can view all linked apps and manage them within your workspace. To link more apps, click **+Link Apps** and select the app from the list. To unlink an app, click the **unlink** icon next to it. [Learn more](../automation/dynamic-routing.md#setting-up-a-dynamic-routing).
