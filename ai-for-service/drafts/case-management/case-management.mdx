Case management provides a systematic approach to tracking, managing, and resolving customer issues from initial contact through closure. The platform offers role-specific dashboards and structured workflows to streamline the entire process.

## Navigation

Go to **Contact Center AI > Case Management**.

## Process Flow

The following diagram shows the case lifecycle from creation to analysis.

![Process Flow](/ai-for-service/drafts/case-management/images/process-flow-diagram.png)

| Phase | Description |
|---|---|
| **Customer Creation** | A customer contacts the service team with an issue or request. Representatives capture and document relevant information during this initial interaction. |
| **Case Categorization** | The issue is analyzed and classified. Case managers review the documented information to determine the type of issue. |
| **Case Assignment** | Cases are matched with the most appropriate personnel. Team leads consider factors such as expertise, availability, and workload when making assignment decisions. |
| **Investigation and Diagnosis** | Assigned agents conduct thorough investigations to identify the root cause of the customer's issue. |
| **Resolution** | Agents execute the approved resolution plan following established procedures. |
| **Customer Communication** | Representatives maintain regular contact with customers throughout the case lifecycle, bridging internal activities and customer satisfaction. |
| **Case Closure** | Resolved cases are finalized and documented. Agents verify that all resolution steps are completed before archiving. |
| **Review and Analysis** | Quality assurance teams review closed cases to extract insights and drive continuous improvement. |
