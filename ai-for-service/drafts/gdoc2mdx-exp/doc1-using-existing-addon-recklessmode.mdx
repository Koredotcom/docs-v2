**VOICE INTEGRATION | KORE.AI VOICE GATEWAY**

**Using an ElevenLabs Cloned Voice** 

How to configure a custom cloned voice in your Voice AI Agent via the ElevenLabs integration.

**Overview** 

Kore.ai Voice Gateway supports custom voices cloned in ElevenLabs. To activate a cloned voice for your agent, share your ElevenLabs API credentials with the Kore.ai team. Once integration is complete, you can enable the voice through call control parameters — no redeployment required. \

**Setup Steps**



1. **Raise a support ticket with your credentials** 
    1. Submit a support ticket to the Kore.ai team with your API key. The team will complete the backend integration on your account.
    2. If you prefer not to share sensitive credentials on the Zendesk ticket, you may send them to us via a separate secure email.
2. **Activate via call control parameters** 
    3. Once integration is confirmed, enable the cloned voice in your Voice AI Agent by passing the appropriate call control parameters in your flow configuration. No code changes or redeployment needed.
