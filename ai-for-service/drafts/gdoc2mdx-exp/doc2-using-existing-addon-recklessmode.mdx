### Capture Disposition During Transfer

When the **Capture Disposition During Transfer** setting is enabled, agents and supervisors can tag a disposition to an interaction at the point of transfer, before the conversation is closed.

**To capture a disposition during a transfer:**



1. While handling an active interaction, initiate a transfer using the transfer option in the Live Interaction Pane.
2. When the transfer panel opens, select one or more dispositions from the **Disposition** dropdown. The list is populated based on your account's configured dispositions. \

![alt_text](images/image2.png "image_tooltip")

3. Complete the transfer.
    * If a disposition is selected, it is tagged to the interaction at the point of transfer and stored separately from any closure disposition captured later.
    * If the dropdown is skipped, the transfer proceeds without a disposition.

    **Note:** Disposition selection is optional and does not affect the transfer. Disposition data captured at transfer is available in Analytics and reporting.
