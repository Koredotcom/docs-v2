# Kore.ai Platform Documentation

Build intelligent AI solutions for enterprise productivity, customer service, and process automation.

---

## Products

### [Agent Platform](/agent-platform)
Build intelligent, autonomous AI agents that understand context, take actions, and deliver results at enterprise scale. Create multi-agent systems with orchestration, tools, and knowledge.

**Key capabilities:** Multi-agent orchestration, Tool integrations, RAG/Knowledge, Prompt Studio, Evaluation

---

### [AI for Work](/ai-for-work)
Unified platform for enterprise productivity. Deploy custom and pre-built AI agents that connect to your systems and automate workflows across Microsoft Teams, Slack, and web.

**Key capabilities:** Custom agents, Pre-built solutions (IT Assist, HR Assist), Enterprise knowledge, Multi-channel deployment

---

### [AI for Service](/ai-for-service)
Secure, scalable AI solutions for creating engaging customer experiences across voice and digital channels. Includes Automation AI, Search AI, Contact Center AI, Agent AI, and Quality AI.

**Key capabilities:** Virtual assistants, Contact center, Agent copilot, Quality management, 50+ integrations

---

### [AI for Process](/ai-for-process)
Streamline knowledge-intensive business processes with autonomous AI agents. No-code workflow design with human-in-the-loop capabilities and custom model deployment.

**Key capabilities:** Workflow automation, AI nodes, Human-in-the-loop, Model hub, Fine-tuning

---

## Shared Capabilities

These capabilities are available across products:

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Model Hub** | Use OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Azure, or custom fine-tuned models |
| **Guardrails** | Built-in safety for toxicity, PII, prompt injection, and bias |
| **Analytics** | Unified monitoring, traces, and performance metrics |
| **SSO & Security** | Enterprise authentication, encryption, and compliance |
| **Audit Logging** | Complete activity tracking for compliance requirements |
